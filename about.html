Founder and Director of “EVOLVE MINDSHALA” organisation

He has developed very effective and highly result oriented Signature training and coaching programmes by integrating best learnings and experiences of various human mind technologies mastered by him over so many years. He also keeps upgrading them. His vast knowledge forms the foundation for his powerful trainings through his Workshops, Seminars, Webinars, Counselings, Coachings, Online Sessions, Courses etc. He is very passionate to help people unleash their true potential & succeed in all their personal and professional endeavours. Large number of participants in his various training programmes all over India & abroad have not only been able to walk out of their problems but have seen tremendous transformation in their lives. He is Founder and Director of “EVOLVE MINDSHALA” organisation. He is also writing some books on Mastering Inner Power & unwinding the Powers of Mind which he intends to get published soon.
